TheraCan is your platform for everything related to medical cannabis.

Medicinal cannabis became partially legalized on April 1, 2024, and is now accessible through TheraCan for medical advice on this subject.

1st Step

Fill out the medical history form and describe your needs. Select a doctor partnering with TheraCan to begin treatment for your medical cannabis prescription.

2nd Step

After payment, your selected doctor will contact you by phone or video call for a telemedical consultation, followed by a private prescription if indicated.

3rd Step

Receive your prescription digitally and directly from your doctor to a pharmacy of your choice or a mail-order pharmacy.
